About Me

Who am I?

Hello all! My name is Monineath (MOH-ni-neeth). I know, it is a unique name, isn't it? My grandpa named me after the Queen of Cambodia. This was already a rare name back in my home country, but after coming to the U.S. at the age of 8, I realize this name was even more of "one-of-a-kind".

Having grown up in Minnesota majority of my life, I've learned to pick up hobbies to rotate for winter and summer days. I've dabbled in indoor rock climbing, wheel throwing, sewing, weight lifting, rollerblading & skating, longboarding,and attending music festivals & concerts.

My boyfriend and I moved to the Bay Area in March of 2022 due to him accepting a software engineer position with LinkedIn. His career milstone gave us both an opportunity for a new chapter in our lives. Now that I'm in NorCal, I'm really looking forward to picking up more hobbies, possibly hiking.
